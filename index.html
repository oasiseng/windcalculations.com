Methodology and References

Core wind equations are implemented in-browser for transparency and review. ASCE 7-22 Eq. 26.10-1 for velocity pressure (Kd excluded from qz); Ch. 30 Eq. 30.3-1 for low-rise C&C net pressure (Kd applied in pressure equation). ASCE 7-16 follows the prior form with Kd inside qz.

Applicable scope: Low-rise enclosed / partially enclosed / partially open / open buildings with h ≤ 60 ft. GCp values from Ch. 30 Figures 30.3-1 (walls) and 30.3-2A–D (roofs) depend on effective wind area and zone. Users must verify the applicable figure, zone, and effective wind area for their project.
