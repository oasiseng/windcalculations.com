Methodology and References

Core wind equations are implemented in-browser for transparency and review. ASCE 7-22 Eq. 26.10-1 for velocity pressure (Kd excluded from qz); Ch. 30 Eq. 30.3-1 for low-rise C&C net pressure (Kd applied in pressure equation). ASCE 7-16 follows the prior form with Kd inside qz. Net design pressures are subject to the 16 psf minimum of Section 30.2.2.

Applicable scope: Low-rise enclosed / partially enclosed / partially open / open buildings with h ≤ 60 ft. Wall GCp values from Fig. 30.3-1 (Zones 4 & 5). Roof GCp values from Fig. 30.3-2A–D per ASCE 7-16/7-22 (Zones 1', 1, 2, 3 — substantially higher than ASCE 7-10, with zone geometry now based on building-height offsets (0.6h) and L-shaped Zone 3 corners). GCp depends on effective wind area and zone; users must verify the applicable figure, zone layout, and effective wind area for their project.
