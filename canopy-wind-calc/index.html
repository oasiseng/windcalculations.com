Direct-Bolt Cantilever — Back Anchorage (no-arm)

Back gutter fascia bolts directly to a concrete / CMU beam; the canopy cantilevers out the projection. Wind & dead on the projection create a cantilever moment at the back, resisted by a tension / bearing couple across the anchor row plus vertical shear. Set anchor count, diameter & embedment in Connections → 3 (Wall / Substrate Anchor).

As detailed: 24" (2'-0") max.
Bolt line → fascia bearing; ≈ fascia height.

In this mode the panel below labeled Tie-Back Arm checks the 2"×2" tie member as a supplementary axial element (conservatively carries the full tributary reaction — actual sharing with the anchor couple is indeterminate).
