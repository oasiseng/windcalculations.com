Method: Rooftop structures and equipment force form per ASCE 7 Sec. 29.4.1 (buildings h ≤ 60 ft): Fh = qh × (GCr) × Af and Fv = qh × (GCr) × Ar, with Kd placed per the selected edition (inside qh for 7-16; in the force equation for 7-22). Default GCr values are the conservative Sec. 29.4.1 caps (1.9 lateral, 1.5 uplift). Stability screening treats the unit as a rigid body; anchorage design (embedment, hardware, curb/deck capacity) is outside this tool's scope.
