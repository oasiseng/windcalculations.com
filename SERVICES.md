# Services

WindCalculations.com is supported by Oasis Engineering LLC, a licensed professional engineering firm providing wind load analysis and structural consulting services.

While this repository hosts a public wind parameter calculator, professional engineering services are available for projects requiring formal documentation, review, or sealed deliverables.

---

## 1. Wind Load Calculations

We provide site-specific wind load analysis in accordance with:

- ASCE 7 (current adopted edition)
- Florida Building Code (FBC)
- International Building Code (IBC)
- Local jurisdictional amendments

Services include:

- Determination of basic wind speed (Vult / Vasd)
- Exposure classification
- Velocity pressure (qz, qh)
- Components & cladding pressures
- Main wind force resisting system (MWFRS) pressures
- Rooftop equipment and HVAC anchorage pressures
- Solar panel wind load analysis
- Sign and screen enclosure wind pressures

---

## 2. Engineering Letters & Sealed Reports

For permit submittals and AHJ review, we offer:

- Signed and sealed wind pressure reports
- Rooftop HVAC anchorage verification letters
- Equipment wind compliance documentation
- Structural adequacy letters
- Site-specific wind parameter certifications

All sealed documents are prepared and reviewed by a licensed Professional Engineer.

## 3. Permit Support & Documentation

We assist contractors, architects, and owners with:

- Responding to wind-related plan review comments
- Clarifying wind design criteria for permits
- Supporting documentation for equipment replacement permits
- High-Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ) wind design support

## 4. Structural Consulting

In addition to wind calculations, Oasis Engineering provides:
