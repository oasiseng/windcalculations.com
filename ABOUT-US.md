# About WindCalculations.com

**WindCalculations.com** is a public engineering reference tool and calculation suite dedicated to providing transparent, code-aligned wind load data for structural analysis and permitting support.

This project is developed and maintained as part of **Oasis Engineering**, a licensed professional engineering firm based in Tampa, Florida, specializing in structural engineering, wind load analysis, and permit compliance services.

## Our Mission

Wind load calculations are fundamental to safe and compliant building design, especially in regions exposed to high wind events, hurricanes, and extreme weather.  
Our mission is to elevate access to **transparent, reproducible wind pressure data that engineers, designers, contractors, and AHJs can reference with confidence.**

We aim to:
- **Demystify wind load calculations** by exposing intermediate values, formulas, and assumptions.
- **Provide a public, client-side calculator** that implements ASCE 7-based wind pressure methods without hidden logic.
- Support engineering professionals and permitting stakeholders with reproducible, inspectable results.

## What This Tool Is

This repository hosts a **static, client-side wind pressure calculator** based on ASCE 7 wind load methodology.  
It includes:
- A velocity and reference pressure calculator (e.g., qz/qh)
- Components & cladding net pressure outputs
- Intermediate factors such as exposure, coefficients, and formula breakdowns
- A transparent, explainable UI suitable for use in engineering documentation

The tool is intentionally:
- **Server-less (no backend required)**
- **Reproducible and inspectable**
- **Free of user tracking and authentication barriers**
